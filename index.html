How to Make Money on Whop with Clipping: A Beginner’s Guide

In today’s digital world, platforms like Whop have opened up new ways to earn money online. One trending method gaining popularity is clipping—a unique way to earn by helping others discover great digital products and communities. If you're looking for a side hustle or even a full-time gig, here’s everything you need to know about how to make money on Whop through clipping.

What is Whop?

Whop is an all-in-one digital marketplace where creators and entrepreneurs sell digital products, tools, and memberships. It offers everything from trading communities and productivity tools to online courses and software. What makes Whop stand out is its affiliate-friendly model and “clipping” feature—designed to reward users who help drive sales.

What is Clipping on Whop?

Clipping on Whop is similar to affiliate marketing, but with a twist. When you “clip” a product or community on Whop, you’re essentially bookmarking it and sharing your unique link. When someone buys through your clipped link, you earn a commission. It’s that simple.

Think of it like curating the best tools, communities, or digital content in one place, then earning when others buy through your recommendations.

Why Clipping is a Great Way to Make Money

  • No upfront cost – You don’t need to buy the products to clip them.
  • Low barrier to entry – No experience required. Just share and earn.
  • Passive income potential – Once your link is shared, you can earn from it over time.
  • Built-in marketplace – Whop has hundreds of trending products with built-in affiliate tools.

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Money with Whop Clipping

1. Create a Whop Account

Go to Whop.com and sign up. It’s free to join. Once you’re in, you can start browsing products and communities.

2. Browse the Marketplace

Use the “Explore” tab to discover top-performing communities, tools, and subscriptions. Categories include:

  • Crypto & trading groups
  • Online courses
  • Bots and software tools
  • Productivity apps
  • Fitness programs
  • E-commerce resources

3. Clip Products You Like

When you find a product you think people will find valuable, click the “Clip” button. This adds it to your “clipped” section and generates a unique affiliate link for you.

4. Promote Your Clipped Links

Share your links on platforms like:

  • YouTube – Review or recommend digital tools.
  • TikTok – Post short clips highlighting benefits.
  • Instagram – Share via stories or carousel posts.
  • Pinterest – Create pins around "Top 5 Tools" and link your clips.
  • Twitter (X) – Post deals or trending tools.
  • Blog – Write listicles or product reviews.
  • Reddit and Discord – Add value before dropping links.

5. Track Your Earnings

In your Whop dashboard, you can monitor clicks, sales, and commissions in real time. This helps you see what’s working.

Tips to Increase Your Earnings

  • Focus on a niche: Build trust and authority in a specific area.
  • Create consistent content: More content = more reach.
  • Use SEO: Optimize content with relevant keywords.
  • Offer bonuses: Include exclusive extras with your links.
  • Stay updated: Clip new and trending products early.

How Much Can You Earn?

Your earnings vary by strategy. Many Whop creators earn $100 to $1,000+ monthly. With 20–50% commissions and high-ticket offers, there’s serious potential.

Final Thoughts

Clipping on Whop is a smart, beginner-friendly way to make money online—especially if you enjoy discovering and sharing useful digital products. You don’t need to be a marketing expert or have a big following to start. With the right niche, consistent content, and a value-first approach, you can turn your clipped links into a steady stream of income.
